Engineering Rule Book

A list of rules for construction engineers:

  • Rule 1 - Your job is to make money. By building it safely, building it once and leaving a positive legacy.
  • Rule 2 - When faced with opposing views, often the answer is both.
  • Rule 3 - Give me good news, give bad news. Just don’t give me surprises.
  • Rule 4 - Relationships, say hello because success is largely a result of those around you.
  • Rule 5 - The early bird catches the worm, and enjoys the fruits of its labor.
  • Rule 6 - Respect the 6 Ps.
  • Rule 7 - Do not walk past it.
  • Rule 8 - Keep one eye open and your ear to the ground.
  • Rule 9 - Always start with the end in mind.
  • Rule 10 - Pop quiz, what is the number?
